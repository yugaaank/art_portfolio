Portrait Study

Category: Portrait
Year: 2024
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 18" × 24"

Description

This intimate portrait study focuses on the subtle interplay of light and shadow across the human form. The careful attention to detail in the facial features and the dramatic lighting create a sense of depth and personality that draws the viewer into a moment of quiet contemplation.

Executed in traditional oil painting techniques, this piece demonstrates classical portraiture methods while incorporating contemporary sensibilities. The warm color palette and soft brushwork create an atmosphere of serenity and introspection.

Technique

Using a limited palette of earth tones and careful glazing techniques, this portrait was built up in layers over several sessions. The approach emphasizes the timeless quality of oil painting while exploring modern concepts of light and form.
