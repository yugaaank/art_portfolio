Mountain Serenity

Category: Landscape
Year: 2023
Medium: Watercolor
Dimensions: 30" × 20"

Description

This peaceful landscape captures the tranquil beauty of mountain ranges at sunset. The soft, flowing watercolor technique creates an atmosphere of serenity and natural harmony, inviting the viewer to pause and appreciate the quiet majesty of the natural world.

The piece explores the relationship between light and landscape, using the transparency of watercolor to create luminous effects that mirror the gentle transition from day to evening in mountainous terrain.

Location

Inspired by the Sierra Nevada mountain range, this painting was created from studies made during a week-long painting expedition. The golden hour light and atmospheric perspective capture the essence of these majestic peaks.
